about

I was born in Hawaii and spent my early years there before moving to California. My family finally settled in Florida my last year of middle school. I am studying Computer Engineering at Florida Atlanic University. I am interested in AI /machine learning along with edge and embedded systems development.



experience

The first programming language I studied at the university was C++. To further my knowledge or C++ and hardware I began to make Arduino projects. Putting together different Arduino projects have helped me hone my programming skills. I put together a lamp that used a distances sensor to turn on and off as well as adjust the brightness. To test if the plants in my room had enough water, I used a moisture sensor and an Arduino. It displayed the moister in the soil on a small LCD.

Outside of formal education I have perused machine learning and AI. I have made various projects using both Tensorflow and Pytorch. Both of those are currently widely used machine learning frameworks. I wrote the code for a website that would allow a user to update an image and then it would classify that image as a cat or a bird. For the frontend of the website, I used html/css/bootstrap and for the backend I used Python Flask. I stored the images and their classification in a database. Another project I worked on was using reinforcement learning to play a game I created using the Unreal Engine. The AI would have to move a cube around and collect cubes that moved at random. There was a time limit for the game. The reinforcement agent would be feed information to improve its actions over time.
